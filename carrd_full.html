Exhibit Your Rig

WOAH - you got a MAGNET?!

Why did I get a magnet?It's our little way of saying 'hello'!While we're traveling, we always have vans and other rigs catch our eye. Since we don't always have time to say 'hello', we like to leave something so the owners can reach out if they'd like.Stickers were, of course, the easiest idea. But let's face it -- it's kind of rude to put a sticker on someone's van!So, we went with magnets. Easy to leave on a rig, doesn't harm the paint, and is re-usable!So, what do I do?Well, you don't HAVE to do anything! You can just enjoy the free magnet. 😊
BUT If you'd like a 100% free camping ticket to any VanFest event, then you're in luck!Just throw up a post on your social media channel of choice (we prefer Instagram, but we're also on Facebook) of you with your NICE RIG! magnet. It could be you holding it, on your van, whatever! We are not on Twitter/X or Tiktok.Get creative with it! Just make sure to mention us, like "@vanfestusa loves our van/bus/rig!"

Okay, now what?You're done! Once you tag us on a post, we'll reply with a discount code for a free ticket to ANY VanFest event (check out our events here).Why do we this?Because we love our vanlife/nomadic community, and we want to give back any way we can!I got a card, but no magnet!Ahhh, sorry! We probably ran out of magnets, but still had some cards to leave behind. Hit us up on Instagram or shoot us an email and we'll mail a magnet out to you! 😊I have an issue!No worries! Just shoot us a message on Instagram. If you don't have social media, or your code isn't working, just shoot us a quick email (or fill out the form below) and we'll get it squared away. :)

Exhibit your Rig

Your van is more than just a vehicle; it's a vision. Share its magic at VanFest!

You may be asking yourself some questions, like..

What VanFest is All AboutWhile our story started in 2016 when we stumbled upon Eamon & Beck's YouTube videos, it didn't really pick up speed until went to a Tiny Home festival on a whim. It changed everything.

It was the two dozen vans and buses that opened their doors and gave us an actual real-world, tangible feel for what a nomadic life must be like -- and how attainable it truly is.

Five years later, we've been on the road full-time for over three years, and traversed across the US at least five times. We've met so many amazing people in this truly unique and inspiring community, and we're working to make sure that VanFest can inspire others.Why Exhibit Your Van?Ever pulled up somewhere and had someone's eyes light up at the sight of your van? Maybe they’re curious about how you managed to fit a cozy bed, a kitchen, and all those clever storage solutions inside. Or perhaps they’re dreaming of hitting the open road themselves, taking photos of rigs and getting inspired for their own nomadic journey. VanFest provides the opportunity to give them a sneak peek into the world of vanlife and show them that it can be a reality for everyone.---FAQs for ExhibitorsWhat does exhibiting your rig entail?On the Saturday and Sunday of a VanFest event, we encourage everyone to open their doors to the public (as well as other nomads) during certain hours to make new friends and help others get a glimpse into what a nomadic life is all about. You’ll get to teach others about your rig, share stories from the road, and inspire the next generation of nomads and vanlifers. Exhibitors make VanFest the amazing event that it is, and we (literally) wouldn’t be here without amazing people like you!Do I HAVE to showcase/exhibit my rig?Well, no one's twisting your arm! But come on, where's the fun in keeping all that awesomeness to yourself? There's something magical about showing someone around your home-on-wheels for the first time. Plus, think back to before you started this journey. Remember the excitement, the intrigue, maybe even a touch of envy when you first peeked inside a lived-in van? That’s the magic of VanFest. And by sharing, you're not just flaunting design skills; you're giving back to a community that thrives on shared experiences and collective wisdom.Perks of Being an Exhibitor:- Prime Parking: First come, first parked – right at the heart of the action!
- Social Media Shoutout: Let's get you and your van in the spotlight.
- Raffle Ready: Bag yourself a free raffle ticket (one per rig) for some great items from our sponsors and vendors!
- Best in Show Contest: Think your rig's got what it takes? Join in and you might just drive away with a title!
- Photo-Op: Get professional pics of your rig – the high-res digital copies are yours to keep/use how you'd like
- Stay Ahead: Early bird registration for any future VanFest event.How to Exhibit at VanFest1. Grab Your Ticket: Secure your spot in the showcase by grabbing your ticket early. Spaces are limited!2. Prep Your Rig: This doesn’t mean a full makeover! Just ensure it’s tidy, safe, and ready to welcome guests.3. Arrive and Shine: Park in the designated exhibitor area, set up any displays or information you'd like to share, and get ready to be the talk of the festival!

About VanFest

VANFEST IS FOR THE

Van Curious™

VanFest is for the van curious™ among us. It’s a haven for those who are interested in the journey into the heart of adventure, and a source for creative inspiration for the nomadic dreamers. At VanFest, a diverse array of converted vans, buses, and other custom homes open up a world of possibilities, offering a tangible glimpse into the vanlife for attendees. From those who’ve lived the life on the open road to the ones who’ve admired van builds from afar, everyone is invited to dive deep, discover, and connect at VanFest.We're not merely about parading van builds; it’s an immersive educational and community-driven adventure! VanFest brings to life practical workshops, engaging classes, and enlightening talks on topics like DIY conversions, digital nomadism, lifestyle choices, wellness on the road, and beyond. It's is a rich source of knowledge and guidance for those yearning to embrace the nomadic way of living.

AND FOR THE

Nomad Community

VanFest is a celebration of the nomadic community.
