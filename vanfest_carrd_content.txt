VanFest Community Media Program

VanFest believes that sharing is caring, and we want to create a space where everyone can celebrate and relive the incredible experiences that make our events so special. That’s why we’re excited to introduce the VanFest Community Media Program—a way for all attendees to contribute and access amazing photos and videos from our gatherings!This initiative is designed to bring the VanFest community closer by making it easy to share event moments, inspiring others to embrace the vanlife movement and experience the magic of life on the road. Whether it’s a stunning sunset over a van-lined campground, an unforgettable workshop, or just the joy of meeting fellow nomads, your captured moments help tell the VanFest story.How It WorksThrough this program, anyone who attends VanFest can upload their pictures and videos to our Community Media Folder, where they will be freely available for others in the community to use for:📌 Social media posts that showcase the VanFest experience

📌 Event recaps to highlight key moments and memories

📌 VanFest-specific promotions that help grow and strengthen our communityGuidelines for UseBy uploading to the Community Media Folder, you agree to the following:✅ Open Sharing – Anyone in the community can use the uploaded media for non-commercial purposes related to VanFest.

✅ No Selling – Your contributions will not be sold (e.g., as prints, stock photos, or for commercial resale).

✅ Respectful Use – All media should be used in a positive way that respects VanFest, its attendees, sponsors, vendors, and the greater vanlife community.

✅ Agreement to Terms – Before uploading, please review the full usage agreement on our Terms page.Why Participate?The VanFest community is built on connection and collaboration. By contributing, you’re helping to create an ever-growing archive of vanlife adventures that inspire and connect both newcomers and seasoned nomads alike. Whether you're looking for the perfect photo to share your VanFest experience or just want to reminisce about the good times, this program ensures those memories live on.✨ Let’s capture, share, and celebrate the magic of VanFest together! ✨🚀 Ready to contribute? Upload your media below! 📸
